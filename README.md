# DLW_DNA

AI Safety Hivemind — a two-sided public safety intelligence system.

Inbound (Human Sensors): Anyone can call or message in any language. The system transcribes, extracts incident type, severity, and GPS location, then automatically routes it to the right agency with a structured incident card.

Outbound (Environmental Sensors): Historical crime data, weather, and time-of-day patterns generate a live risk map. When a zone's risk score spikes, agencies get a preemptive caution before anything is even reported.

The loop: A report in an already high-risk zone gets auto-escalated in severity. Residents nearby get an SMS advisory. Agencies get full context. Everything is pinned to location, so human reports and predictive signals reinforce each other.

One system, two signal sources, closes the gap between what people report and what data already knows.
